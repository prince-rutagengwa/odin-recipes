A well prepared chicken

this lasagna is prepared by the best cook in kigali-Rwanda, and it is so delicious, you should try it.

Ingredients

  • 750 grams chicken
  • 4 tablespoon mustard oil
  • 2 cup onion chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato minced
  • 0 As required salt

Steps to prepare the recipe

  1. Wash and clean the chicken
  2. Saute whole spices
  3. Add spices in frying chicken
  4. cook for 2-4 minutes with closed lid and serve hot

A home made pizza

this is a classic homemade pizza recipe, including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough: Makes enough dough for two 10-12 inch pizzas
  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490 g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

steps to prepare a homemade pizza

  1. proof the yeast
  2. make and knead the pizza dough
  3. let the dough rise
